Arduino Workshop
Want to head into a world of electronics and programming?? Still confused where to begin from?? Why start low?? Can't you just begin by making a robot which is controlled by your gestures?? TESLA'20 is hosting a Workshop on Gesture controlled bot using Arduino on 25th January at College of Engineering, Trivandrum Arduino is an open-source electronics platform based on easy-to-use hardware and software. Arduino boards are able to read inputs - light on a sensor, a finger on a button, or a Twitter message - and turn it into an output - activating a motor, turning on an LED, publishing something online. You can tell your board what to do by sending a set of instructions to the microcontroller on the board.
Components provided:
Arduino Uno with cable
HC-05 Bluetooth Module
L293D Motor-driver
DC Motors(2)
Potentiometer(1 kohm)
Resistors - 1000 ohm(5 nos.)
LEDs (10nos.)
Switchbutton(1)
Jumper wires - Male-Female(15), Male-Male(15),female -female(6)
9V Battery(2)
2-wheel Robot Chassis
Wheels - 2
Caster wheel
Snapper with jack(1)
Snapper without jack(1)
Breadboard(1)
The topics that would be covered are:
1. Introduction to arduino(With Blink Program)
2. Pwm And analog(led brightness control with analogwrite, potentiometer,
3. Ultrasonic and ir sensor
4. Motor control using l293d
5. HC05 Module(Bluetooth gesture controlled bot)
